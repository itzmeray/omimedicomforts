RKR OMI MediComforts

MediComforts specializes in the manufacturing of Disposable Surgery Packs, Pre-designed Drapes, and Wraps for healthcare organizations. Our main goal is to understand the dynamic nature of the markets and deliver products accordingly.

Our range of products are:

  • Surgeon Gown
  • Patient Gown / OT Table Sheet / Hole Towel / Hole Drape
  • Disposable Bed Sheet and Pillow Covers
  • PPE Kit
  • General Surgery Drape Kit / Major / Minor
  • LSCS / Laparoscopic / Delivery Kits
  • HIV Kit / TKR / Ortho Kit

We also offer all kinds of customization based on customer requirements. Established in 2019, the company has its corporate office in Bangalore and branch office in Gadag. Our raw material suppliers are certified with ISO, CE, and AAMI standards. We provide sterilization reports for all batches.
